Privacy Policy
Last updated: 2026

Your files

DECK converts video to audio entirely inside your browser. The video file you select or drop onto the page is never uploaded, transmitted, or sent to any server — including ours. We have no way to see, store, or access the files you convert.

What we don't collect

We do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • The video or audio files you convert
  • Any personal information you might have on your device
  • Account data, since there are no accounts on this site

Hosting & basic logs

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Like most web hosts, GitHub may automatically log standard technical information (such as IP address and browser type) for security and performance purposes. This is outside our control — see GitHub's own privacy statement for details.

Advertising

This site may display third-party advertisements (such as through Google AdSense) to help cover hosting costs. These ad providers may use cookies or similar technologies to serve relevant ads and measure performance. You can control ad personalization through your browser settings or via Google's Ad Settings.

Donations

If you choose to use the coffee/tip link on this site, you'll be taken to a third-party platform (Ko-fi) which has its own separate privacy policy and handles any payment information directly — we never see or store your payment details.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated occasionally as the site changes. Check back here if you have questions about how it works.
